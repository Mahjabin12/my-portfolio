How I work

A short loop,
repeated until it's right.

Every project moves through the same four moves — fast enough to stay curious, slow enough to stay honest. The artefact at the end is just evidence the loop worked.

01

Discover

Listen, observe, frame the real problem.

02

Define

Turn signals into a sharp design brief.

03

Design

Sketch, prototype, system the surface.

04

Validate

Test with people, ship, learn, repeat.
