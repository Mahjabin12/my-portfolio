Verification Guidelines

Mandatory Rejection

  • Expired medicines
  • Opened/tampered packaging
  • No batch number
  • Temperature excursion
  • Counterfeit suspicions

Approval Criteria

  • Valid expiry (min 3 months)
  • Original sealed packaging
  • Clear batch number
  • Proper storage conditions
  • Legible labeling

Special Attention

  • Refrigerated medicines
  • Controlled substances
  • High-value medicines
  • Near-expiry medicines
  • Large quantity donations
