Popular Tourist Destinations

Discover the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Chittagong

Sea Beach

Patenga Beach

Patenga Beach is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Chittagong. Located at the mouth of the Karnaphuli River where it meets the Bay of Bengal, this beautiful beach offers stunning views, especially during sunset. The beach is always bustling with visitors enjoying the sea breeze, local street food, and the scenic beauty.

  • 📍 Location: 14 km from city center
  • ⏰ Best Time: Evening (4-7 PM) for sunset
  • 💰 Entry Fee: Free
  • 🚗 How to Reach: CNG/Taxi from city (30-40 min)

Nearby Hotels & Restaurants:

Lake & Amusement Park

Foy's Lake

Foy's Lake is a man-made lake and popular tourist spot featuring an amusement park, water world, and resort. Named after Mr. Foy, the British railway engineer who excavated it in 1924, the lake is surrounded by hills and offers various recreational activities. The amusement park includes rides, restaurants, and beautiful gardens.

  • 📍 Location: 8 km north of city center
  • ⏰ Opening Hours: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • 💰 Entry Fee: 50-200 Tk (depending on activities)
  • 🎢 Activities: Boating, rides, zip-line, water park

Nearby Hotels & Restaurants:

Foy's Lake Resort Hill View Restaurant Lakeside Café
Religious & Historical

Bayazid Bostami Shrine

The shrine of Bayazid Bostami is located on a hillock in Nasirabad area. This revered site includes a mosque, tomb, and a pond with hundreds of sacred turtles. According to legend, these turtles are transformed followers of the saint. The location offers spiritual tranquility and panoramic views of the city.

  • 📍 Location: Nasirabad, 7 km from city
  • ⏰ Best Time: Morning or evening
  • 💰 Entry Fee: Free (donations welcome)
  • 🐢 Special: Sacred pond with turtles

Nearby Hotels & Restaurants:

Nasirabad Inn Heritage Restaurant
Religious & Hill

Chandranath Hill Temple

Chandranath Hill, located in Sitakunda, is one of the most sacred Hindu pilgrimage sites in Bangladesh. The ancient temple sits atop the hill at 1,020 feet, dedicated to Goddess Chandranath. The location offers breathtaking panoramic views and is particularly crowded during the Shiva Chaturdashi festival in March.

  • 📍 Location: Sitakunda, 37 km from Chittagong
  • ⏰ Best Time: Early morning for sunrise
  • 💰 Entry Fee: Free
  • 🥾 Trek: 1-2 hour climb (steps available)

Nearby Hotels & Restaurants:

Sitakunda Rest House Hill View Dhaba
River & Scenic

Karnaphuli River

The Karnaphuli River is the lifeline of Chittagong, flowing through the heart of the city before emptying into the Bay of Bengal. The river offers spectacular views, especially from Sadarghat area. Boat rides on the Karnaphuli provide a unique perspective of the port city, with ships, boats, and the cityscape creating a memorable experience.

  • 📍 Main Viewing: Sadarghat, Kalurghat Bridge
  • ⏰ Best Time: Sunset (5-6:30 PM)
  • 💰 Boat Ride: 100-500 Tk (negotiable)
  • 🚤 Activities: River cruises, boat rides

Nearby Hotels & Restaurants:

Riverside Hotel Port View Restaurant
Museum & Culture

Ethnological Museum

The Chittagong Ethnological Museum showcases the rich cultural heritage of the tribal communities of Bangladesh. Established in 1965, it houses artifacts, traditional costumes, handicrafts, and exhibits representing the lifestyle of various indigenous groups including Chakma, Marma, Tripura, and others.

  • 📍 Location: Agrabad, city center
  • ⏰ Opening Hours: 10 AM - 5 PM (Closed Friday)
  • 💰 Entry Fee: 20 Tk (Bangladeshi), 100 Tk (Foreign)
  • 🎨 Collections: 5000+ artifacts from 25+ tribes
