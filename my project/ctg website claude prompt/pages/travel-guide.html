Complete Travel Guide

Everything you need to know for a perfect trip to Chittagong

✈️ How to Reach Chittagong

Chittagong is well-connected to major cities in Bangladesh and has international connectivity. Here are your options:

✈️

By Air

Shah Amanat International Airport (CGP) is the main airport serving Chittagong.

  • From Dhaka: 45 minutes flight
  • Airlines: Biman, Novoair, US-Bangla
  • Frequency: Multiple daily flights
  • International: Direct flights to Middle East
  • Airport Distance: 12 km from city
🚂

By Train

Comfortable and scenic journey through Bangladesh countryside.

  • Subarna Express: Dhaka-CTG (5-6 hrs)
  • Turna Nishitha: Night train option
  • Mahanagar Godhuli: AC chair option
  • Station: Chittagong Railway Station
  • Fare: 400-1200 Tk
🚌

By Bus

Economical option with multiple operators and departure times.

  • From Dhaka: 5-7 hours
  • Operators: Greenline, Shyamoli, Hanif
  • Fare: 500-1500 Tk
  • Terminals: Oxygen Mor, Bahaddarhat
  • AC/Non-AC: Both available

🚗 Local Transportation

Getting around Chittagong is easy with various transportation options:

🛺

CNG Auto-rickshaw

Most popular and convenient for short to medium distances.

  • Available everywhere 24/7
  • Fare: 30-200 Tk
  • Metered service
  • Can fit 3 passengers
🚕

Taxi / Ride-sharing

Uber and Pathao available for comfortable rides.

  • App-based booking
  • AC vehicles
  • Fixed pricing
  • Safe and reliable
🚌

City Buses

Cheapest option for budget travelers.

  • Extensive routes
  • Fare: 10-30 Tk
  • Can be crowded
  • Local experience

🌤️ Best Time to Visit & Weather

Chittagong has a tropical wet climate with distinct seasons. Plan your visit according to your preferences:

🌸 Winter (Nov-Feb)

15-25°C

Best Time! Pleasant weather, clear skies, perfect for sightseeing. Ideal for beach visits and outdoor activities.

☀️ Summer (Mar-May)

25-35°C

Hot and humid. Good for hill stations. Carry sunscreen and stay hydrated. Less crowded tourist spots.

🌧️ Monsoon (Jun-Sep)

24-32°C

Heavy rainfall, lush greenery. Beautiful landscapes but travel can be challenging. Not recommended for beach trips.

🍂 Autumn (Oct-Nov)

20-30°C

Pleasant weather begins, clear skies after monsoon. Great for photography and outdoor activities.

💡 Important Travel Tips

💰

Money & Banking

ATMs are widely available. Carry cash for small purchases. Major hotels and restaurants accept cards. Currency exchange available at banks and airports.

📱

Mobile & Internet

Get a local SIM card (Grameenphone, Robi, Banglalink). Available at airport and city centers. 4G coverage is good. Free WiFi at major hotels and cafes.

👕

Dress Code

Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites. Light cotton clothes for summer. Carry a light jacket for winter evenings. Comfortable walking shoes recommended.

🍽️

Food Safety

Drink bottled water only. Try street food from busy stalls. Wash hands before eating. Restaurants in hotels and tourist areas are generally safe. Ask for less spicy if not accustomed.

🎒

What to Pack

Sunscreen, insect repellent, comfortable shoes, power adapter, first-aid kit, umbrella, photocopies of important documents, and local guidebook.

📸

Photography

Ask permission before photographing people. Avoid restricted areas (military zones, airports). Best photos at Patenga Beach sunset and hilltop viewpoints.

🗣️ Essential Bangla Phrases

Greetings

Hello - আসসালামু আলাইকুম (Assalamu Alaikum)

Thank you - ধন্যবাদ (Dhonnobad)

Please - দয়া করে (Doya kore)

How much? - কত টাকা? (Koto taka?)

Directions

Where is...? - ...কোথায়? (Kothay?)

Left - বাম (Bam)

Right - ডান (Dan)

Straight - সোজা (Shoja)

Emergencies

Help! - সাহায্য! (Shahajjo!)

Hospital - হাসপাতাল (Hashpatal)

Police - পুলিশ (Police)

I need help - আমার সাহায্য দরকার (Amar shahajjo dorkar)
